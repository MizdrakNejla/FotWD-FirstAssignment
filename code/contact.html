How to Fill Out the Form

1. Name and Surname: Please enter your first and last name accurately.

2. Email: Use a valid email address so we can reach you.

3. Phone: Enter your phone number in the format (1234567890) for contact purposes.

4. Message: Provide a brief description of your inquiry or project.

5. Upload an Image: You can drag and drop an image or select one manually to provide visual references.

6. Service Selection: Choose the service you are interested in (Consultation or Renovation).

7. Preferred Contact Method: Choose whether you'd prefer to be contacted via email or phone.

8. Preferred Contact Time: Select a date and time that works best for you to be contacted.

9. Terms and Conditions: You must agree to the terms before submitting the form.
